About Me

A not-so-long story

My name is Alina, and I'm currently pursuing a Master in Public Policy degree at the Harvard Kennedy School of Government. I graduated from Westminster College, PA in 2018, with a degree in International Politics. In my previous life I was a GIS geek and local government web developer, where I helped build this site and others from the ground up in my hometown of Buffalo, New York.
